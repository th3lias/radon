The \(d\)--dimensional Radon transform of a function \(f: \R^d \to \R\) is defined as
\[
    \rad f(\sigma, \theta) \defn \intt{\inner{x}{\theta} = \sigma}{}{f(x)}{x},\qquad (\sigma, \theta) \in \R \times \S^{d-1}.
\]
